## Production Settings for Wan2.2

### T2V Optimal Settings:
- **Resolution:** 768×768 or 1024×576 (landscape)
- **Frames:** 48-72 (2-3 seconds at 24fps)
- **Motion Strength:** 0.6-0.8 (controlled professional motion)
- **CFG Scale:** 4-6 (balanced guidance)
- **Seed:** Fixed for consistency across variations

### I2V Optimal Settings:
- **Motion Strength:** 0.4-0.7 (subtle refined motion)
- **Reference Strength:** 0.8-0.9 (maintain product appearance)
- **Frames:** 24-48 (1-2 seconds)
- **Smoothness:** Maximum (avoid stuttering)

## Marketing Content Workflow

### 1. Hero Shots (T2V)
Generate 3-5 second product reveals with dramatic lighting and motion

### 2. Feature Details (I2V)
Start with static renders, add subtle motion to highlight features

### 3. Lifestyle Context (T2V)
Create usage scenarios with environmental context

### 4. Technical Specs (I2V)
Animate component callouts and specification reveals

### 5. Final Edit
Combine clips with professional editing software, add:
- Brand graphics overlays
- Voiceover or music
- Text callouts for specifications
- Call-to-action endings
